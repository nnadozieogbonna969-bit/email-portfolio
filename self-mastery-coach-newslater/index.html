The Identity Trap: Why You Keep Repeating Old Patterns

Most people believe that changing their habits will change their life. But the research — and my experience coaching hundreds of clients — tells a different story. It is your identity, not your habits, that drives everything.

When you believe "I am not confident," no amount of rehearsal before an interview will fix the root issue. When you believe "I am not worthy of success," you will unconsciously sabotage every opportunity that reaches your door.

Self mastery begins the moment you stop managing your behavior and start questioning your beliefs.

Journaling Prompt

Your 5-Minute Mastery Practice

Set a timer for 5 minutes. In your journal, answer this one question with full honesty:

“What belief about myself am I most afraid to let go of — and why?”

Do not edit. Do not judge. Just write. The answer will reveal exactly where your next breakthrough lives.
